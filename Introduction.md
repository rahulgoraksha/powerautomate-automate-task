## What is Power Automate Flow?
### What is a flow?
- It is a visual representation of any task (Trigger-Action)
- Trigger: activate the workflow
- Action: series of steps followed after Trigger

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
|Email    | Add row in excel|
|Click    | Slack Message|
|Time    | File Movement|
